Welcome to Alston Ridge Middle School's Webpage

This webpage is dedicated to providing some information about my school.

Alston Ridge Middle School is located in 7833 Fussell Avenue, Cary NC 27519. The school has a great learning environment and offers many activities for students. The school mascot is the warrior, and the school colors are navy blue and lemon yellow. In this webpage I can share a little bit more about my school with you and I hope you find it informative!

The school educates students in grades 6-8 and is part of the Wake County Public School System. ARMS offers a variety of academic programs like advanced placement courses, as well as extracurricular activities such as sports, music, and clubs.

Arms was established in 2019 and has quickly transferred students into the best school in the area.

Our vision (straight from the founder)"Alston Ridge Middle School is committed to fostering purposeful relationships, empowering students to be lifelong learners, and creating a sense of belonging through meaningful experiences. "

I hope you found this informative and helpful. If you would like to learn more about Alston Ridge Middle School, please feel free to contact the school directly!

Just 5 steps on how to make Maggi Noodles on the stovetop!

  1. Boil about one and a half cups of water in a pot on high heat
  2. Add the noodle cake into the water with the yummy maggi seasoning packet
  3. Let the noodles cook for 2-3 minutes and stir occasionally
  4. Once the noodles are cooked you can add garnish like an egg or spring onions
  5. Enjoy your delicious Maggi noodles!
